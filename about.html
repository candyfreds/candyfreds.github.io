ABOUT ME

I grew up in Salt Lake City, Utah, where I was exposed to a lot of outdoor adventures and experiences. I learned how to ski, snowboard, rock climb, play sports, scuba dive, and much more at a very young age which gave me the inspiration I needed to create art. Learning graphic design was something that came very easily to me and allowed me to express myself in a more contextual way: helping the people around me understand information visually. For me, it was always difficult learning something new without visual, hands on, or even aditory examples so I wanted to continue helping people in a way that helped me.
